### Pricing Psychology

- **Anchor effect:** Show lifetime price first to make annual feel cheap by
  comparison — OR show annual first to make lifetime feel like a deal
- **A/B test both** via Superwall to find which converts better for your category
- **Lifetime should be 2-3x annual** — less and you're leaving money on the table,
  more and nobody buys it
- **Avoid monthly pricing** for indie apps — it feels nickel-and-dime and churns
  faster. Annual + lifetime is the winning indie combo.
- **Free trial:** 7 days is standard. 3 days for apps with instant value
  (utilities), 14 days for apps that need habit formation (fitness, journaling).

## Retention Benchmarks

Retention is the #1 predictor of long-term revenue. If users don't come back,
nothing else matters.

| Metric     | Poor  | Average | Good   | Great  |
| ---------- | ----- | ------- | ------ | ------ |
| D1 (Day 1) | <20%  | 25-30%  | 35-40% | 45%+   |
| D7         | <10%  | 12-18%  | 20-25% | 30%+   |
| D30        | <5%   | 6-10%   | 12-18% | 20%+   |

### Retention by Category

| Category         | Typical D1 | Typical D30 | Notes                              |
| ---------------- | ---------- | ----------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Social           | 30-40%     | 15-25%      | Network effects drive retention    |
| Health & Fitness | 25-35%     | 8-15%       | Habit formation is hard            |
| Productivity     | 20-30%     | 10-15%      | Depends on daily utility           |
| Games            | 25-35%     | 5-10%       | High initial, drops fast           |
| Utilities        | 15-25%     | 10-20%      | Lower daily use, but sticky        |
| Finance          | 25-35%     | 15-20%      | Transactions create switching cost |

### How to Improve Retention

1. **Onboarding:** Show core value within 60 seconds of first launch
2. **Notifications:** Smart reminders (not spammy) at natural trigger points
3. **Streaks:** Gamify daily usage (but don't punish missed days)
4. **Quick wins:** Let users feel productive in <2 minutes per session
5. **Data lock-in:** The more data users add, the harder it is to leave

### Apple App Store Featuring

Getting featured by Apple is the highest-impact free marketing channel. To
maximize your chances:

1. **Use latest APIs** — Apple loves showcasing new platform capabilities
2. **Fill out privacy nutrition labels** completely and accurately
3. **Implement App Tracking Transparency** properly
4. **Support latest iOS version** features and design patterns
5. **Excellent accessibility** — VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, color contrast
6. **Localize** — apps available in multiple languages get featured more
7. **Submit for consideration** via App Store Connect
8. **Time your updates** around iOS releases and Apple events
9. **Key dates:** Data Privacy Day (Jan 28), Earth Day (Apr 22), Mental Health
   Awareness Month (May), iOS launch (September), holidays (November-December)
