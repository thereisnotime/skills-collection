# Required Information Checklist

Use this file as the intake checklist. Do not calculate or map elster fields until every applicable section has an explicit answer.

## 1. Tax year and filing scope

Ask first:
- Which tax year from 2024 onward is this return for?
- Is the user asking for an estimate only, or also a field-by-field elster mapping?

## 2. Personal and filing status

Collect:
- tax year
- single / married / separated / divorced / widowed
- joint or separate filing if relevant
- children and child benefits if relevant
- church tax status
- federal state only if a source or form requires it
- current address and whether it changed during the year
- bank details only if needed for the practical filing walkthrough

## 3. Employment income

For each job in the year, ask:
- employer name
- start and end dates
- whether a lohnsteuerbescheinigung exists
- gross wages, wage tax, solidarity surcharge, church tax, tax class, and social insurance if not already in the certificate
- whether values were already imported into elster
- reimbursements paid by the employer
- first workplace location
- days worked on site
- one-way commuting distance
- actual public transport costs if they may exceed the distance allowance

## 4. Wage-replacement benefits and similar income

Ask for each applicable benefit:
- benefit type
- start and end dates
- certificate amount
- issuing authority

Examples:
- arbeitslosengeld
- krankengeld
- elterngeld
- kurzarbeitergeld
- insolvenzgeld
- other progression-relevant benefits

## 5. Self-employment, freelance, or business income

Ask whether the user had any of these. If yes, collect:
- activity type
- gross receipts
- deductible business expenses
- profit or loss if already calculated
- whether an eür or other summary already exists
- any advance tax payments

## 6. Rental, leasing, or property income

Ask whether the user had rental or leasing income. If yes, collect:
- property type and location
- gross rent
- deductible expenses
- financing interest
- depreciation if known
- vacancy periods if relevant

## 7. Capital gains and investment income

Ask whether the user had capital income. If yes, collect:
- bank or broker certificates
- whether the institution is german
- whether capital gains tax was already withheld
- taxable capital income amounts from the certificate
- already used saver allowance
- withheld capital gains tax, solidarity surcharge, and church tax
- loss carryovers or loss certificates if any
- whether the user wants the withholding reviewed or corrected

## 8. Foreign income

Ask whether the user had any foreign income or foreign tax. If yes, collect:
- country
- income type
- gross amount
- foreign tax paid
- treaty or exemption information if known

## 9. Pensions and other income

Ask whether the user had:
- pension income
- private sales transactions
- other miscellaneous income
- maintenance or support income that must be declared

## 10. Job-related deductions for employees

### 10.1 Commuting and travel
Collect:
- first workplace
- number of commuting days
- one-way distance
- transport type
- actual public transport cost when relevant
- business trips separate from normal commuting
- reimbursements

### 10.2 Home office by period
Split the year into periods.
For each period, ask:
- start and end date
- shared room, work corner, or dedicated room
- whether work was mainly from home
- whether the user also went to the first workplace on those days
- count of eligible home-office days

### 10.3 Dedicated home office room
If there was a dedicated room, collect:
- room size in square meters
- total apartment or house size
- monthly rent or ownership costs
- utilities or service charges if relevant
- the user's own share of those costs
- whether the room was used almost exclusively for work
- whether it was the center of the professional activity

### 10.4 Work equipment
For each item, ask:
- item description
- purchase date
- gross cost
- professional-use percentage
- whether reimbursed by employer
- whether already claimed in a previous year
- whether it should be depreciated

Examples:
- laptop
- phone
- monitor
- desk
- office chair
- headset
- printer

### 10.5 Software, phone, and internet
Collect:
- software or subscription name
- payment date or period
- total cost
- work-use share if mixed use
- internet and phone costs for the year
- whether the user has evidence for a higher professional share than the simplification rule

### 10.6 Applications and job search
Collect only actual expenses in the selected year:
- application fees
- postage or copies
- interview travel
- phone or platform costs
- other directly related expenses

### 10.7 Education and training
Collect:
- course or program name
- amount paid
- travel and accommodation if relevant
- whether reimbursed

### 10.8 Other employee expenses
Check whether the user had:
- union fees
- professional associations
- bank account fees used for salary receipt
- moving costs with an independently job-related reason
- double household costs
- work clothing or cleaning costs if eligible

## 11. Special expenses and insurance

Collect applicable items such as:
- private liability insurance
- health or long-term care amounts not already fully imported
- retirement contributions not already fully imported
- church tax paid
- donations
- tax-advice costs only if still deductible for the relevant category under current rules

Do not assume that every insurance is deductible. Verify with official sources.

## 12. Household services and tradespeople

Ask whether the user had invoices for:
- cleaning
- gardening
- household help
- maintenance or repair labor
- service-charge statements with deductible labor components

Collect the labor amount actually paid in the selected year.

## 13. Childcare and family-related items

If relevant, collect:
- childcare provider and cost
- tax identification numbers if needed
- support payments
- schooling or education items only when officially relevant

## 14. Extraordinary burdens and special cases

Ask whether the user had:
- disability-related expenses
- medical expenses not reimbursed
- funeral costs
- divorce or maintenance litigation expenses where potentially relevant
- other unusual burdens

## 15. Taxes already paid or withheld

Collect or confirm:
- wage tax already withheld
- capital gains tax already withheld
- solidarity surcharge already withheld
- church tax already withheld
- advance income tax payments
- foreign tax paid

## 16. Document checklist

Before asking again, check whether the user has already uploaded:
- lohnsteuerbescheinigung
- benefit certificates
- broker tax certificates
- rent or utility statements for home office calculations
- invoices for equipment, software, transport, donations, childcare, or household services

Use the document values directly when available.

## Completion rule

The intake is complete only when every applicable section above has an explicit answer. Explicit answers include exact values, none, not applicable, already taxed, already reimbursed, or unknown after checking the relevant document.
