# Procurement Card Reconciliation Supplement

## When Required
A supplement must be filed for any month that includes entertainment expenses (GL codes 6455, 6460, 6470, 6475). One supplement per statement month.

## Form Layout

**Header fields:**
- Cardholder Name
- Statement Date (month/year of the p-card statement)

**Expense table columns:**

| Date | Location | Persons Involved (Name, Title, Company) | Business Purpose | Total |

**Footer:**
- Total (sum of all entertainment expenses for the month)
- Notes
- Cardholder Signature + Date
- Supervisor Approval + Date
- Area VP Approval + Date (required if alcohol was purchased)

## Filing Convention
Supplement PDFs are saved as: `supplement_BASM_YYYY_MM.pdf`
- Example: `supplement_BASM_2026_03.pdf` for March 2026
- Stored in: `{year}/supplements/`

## Data Collection for Each Entertainment Expense
For the supplement table, you need information beyond what's on the receipt:
- **Location**: restaurant/venue name and city
- **Persons Involved**: full name, title, and company/institution for each attendee
- **Business Purpose**: brief description of the meeting's purpose (e.g., "Discuss collaboration on insect genomics project")
