# Example: Booked Call Campaign (AI Ads Coaching)

## Objective

Convert cold traffic into booked strategy calls for an AI-powered ads coaching / done-for-you service.

---

## 1. Avatar (via avatar-extraction)

**Primary Avatar:**

* 35–45 year old service-based business owner
* Running ads already or has tried before
* Spending $1k–$10k/month or wants to
* Has seen inconsistent or poor results

**Current Situation:**

* Ads feel unpredictable
* Lead quality is inconsistent
* Cost per lead is creeping up

**Pain Points:**

* “I know ads should work, but they’re not working for me”
* Wasted budget with little return
* Agencies overpromise, underdeliver

**Desired Outcome:**

* Predictable, scalable lead flow
* Clear system for acquisition
* Confidence in ad performance

**Failed Attempts:**

* DIY ads
* Hiring freelancers
* Generic agency retainers

**Emotional Drivers:**

* Frustration
* Loss of control
* Desire for certainty

---

## 2. Offer (via offer-extraction)

### Offer Angles

1. Fix what’s broken
2. Build predictable acquisition
3. Turn ads into a system

### Dominant Offer

**“We turn unpredictable ad spend into a structured system that produces qualified leads consistently.”**

### Why This, Why Now

* Costs are rising
* Guesswork is getting more expensive
* AI has changed how ads should be run

---

## 3. Awareness (via schwartz-awareness-mapper)

**Stage:** Problem Aware / Solution Aware

**Messaging Rules:**

* Do not oversell results
* Focus on why their ads aren’t working
* Introduce a better way

**Angle Direction:**

* “It’s not your ads — it’s how they’re structured”

---

## 4. Mechanism (via mechanism-builder)

**Failed Method:**
Running ads without a structured system

**New Mechanism:**
A structured acquisition system using:

* message matching
* creative testing
* qualification layers

**Mechanism Name:**
**“The Advertising System Framework”**

**Why It Works:**
Because it aligns message, audience, and funnel instead of treating ads as isolated campaigns.

---

## 5. Big Idea

“Most ads don’t fail because ads don’t work…
they fail because they were never built as a system.”
