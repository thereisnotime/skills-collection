## File Descriptions

### 1. SKILL.md
**Purpose:** Find, bid on, and complete Replit Bounties for paid development work within the platform.
**Workflow:** Monetization skill - earns income through Replit's built-in freelance marketplace.
**Relates to:** Leverages all technical skills; demonstrates portfolio via replit-hello-world and projects.

## Summary

This skill teaches developers to earn money through Replit Bounties. It covers browsing and filtering available bounties by skill and budget, evaluating bounty scope and feasibility before bidding, crafting compelling proposals that stand out, communicating effectively with bounty posters, delivering high-quality work within deadlines, handling revisions and feedback professionally, building reputation through completed bounties, setting appropriate rates for your skill level, and building a portfolio of successful bounty projects. Bounties provide real-world experience and income.
